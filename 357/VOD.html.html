Vodacom Group Limited

Is a leading South African telecommunications company, founded in 1993 as the first Vodafone Group company established on the African continent. Headquartered in Midrand, South Africa, Vodacom provides mobile and fixed connectivity, digital and financial services, and Internet of Things (IoT) technology across multiple African countries .

    Technological Advancements

    Vodacom has been at the forefront of technological innovation in Africa:

    5G Network: Launched in 2020, Vodacom's 5G network is operational in Johannesburg, Pretoria, and Cape Town.

    VodaPay: A digital wallet app offering services like loans, QR payments, and online shopping.

    Vodacom e-School: An online learning platform providing free educational resources to students, reaching over 1.2 million users
